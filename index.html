Guillermo Droppelmann, PhD

Guillermo Droppelmann is a Clinical Researcher and PhD in Health Sciences (Summa Cum Laude) with postdoctoral training in Engineering. He is a scientific leader bridging clinical practice and data science, with a research focus on machine and deep learning applied to musculoskeletal imaging and diagnostics.

He currently serves as Deputy Head of Research at Clínica MEDS and as Lecturer in the PhD Program in Industrial Engineering at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso (PUCV). He is currently pursuing his second doctoral degree — a PhD in Bioengineering at Universidad Hospital Italiano, Buenos Aires, working on multimodal AI modeling of signals and biomarkers in general population and cancer patients.

Research areas

  • Health status with a focus on cardiometabolic diseases and cancer
  • Clinical research methodology, biostatistics and research ethics
